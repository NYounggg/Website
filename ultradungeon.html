ABOUT

ULTRADUNGEON is a high-octane dungeon crawler currently in development using C# in the Godot game engine. It seeks to combine ideas from rogue-likes, with extraction elements. Players will take what loot they have accumulated, and risk it all to take on increasingly challenging dungeons. Be careful, as death in these dungeons will cause you to permanently lose whatever gear you brought with you.


CLASSES

ULTRADUNGEON has three main classes: Mage, Ranger and Melee, each with their own sets of gear. Players can mix and match weapons, armour and perks to create their own unique builds.

CHARACTERS

ULTRADUNGEON will have a unique cast of characters that can be recruited to stay at your base. They will provide services such as selling items, buying items, giving hints and taking quest items in exchange for rare, exclusive gear.


PLANNED FEATURES

  • Procedurally-generated dungeons with randomized layouts, loot and enemies
  • A home base where the player can interact with NPCs
  • A building system, to allow players to decorate their base
  • Peer-to-peer cooperative multiplayer
  • Progress tracker for unlockables
  • A fishing minigame

RELEASE DATE

ULTRADUNGEON has no planned release date as of now. We work on it in our spare time and hope to release it one day.
